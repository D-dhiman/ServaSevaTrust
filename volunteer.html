Join Our Team

At Serva Seva Trust, volunteers are the heart of everything we do.

By volunteering your time and skills, you can help us fulfill our mission of providing mentorship and support to underprivileged students. Whether you have a few hours each week or can dedicate yourself to a long-term project, there are numerous ways to make a meaningful impact.

Join us in our mission to empower the future generation and make education accessible to all.



Why Volunteer With Us?


Every hour you spend volunteering translates into a step forward for a student in need. Overall, volunteering is a rewarding experience that offers numerous benefits, including:

  • Making a Positive Impact: Help create meaningful change in the lives of underprivileged students.

  • Gaining Valuable Experience: Develop new skills and gain experience in education and mentorship.

  • Building Connections: Meet like-minded individuals and expand your network.

  • Personal Growth: Volunteering can be a fulfilling way to give back and find purpose.

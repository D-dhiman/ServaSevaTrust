Our Work



What originated as a scholarship initiative has blossomed into a comprehensive approach to the development of children. Beyond financial support, the trust now offers mentorship, medical camps, extracurricular activities, and academic sessions.

Over the years, Sarva Sewa Trust has made a significant impact, aiding over 10,000 children in their educational pursuits.




Scholarships


Sarva Sewa Trust, since its inception in 1985, has been dedicated to providing scholarships to deserving children. What began as a core initiative has expanded over the years, supporting students financially to pursue their educational goals. The trust recognizes the pivotal role that financial assistance plays in unlocking the potential of bright young minds.



Mentorship


Going beyond financial aid, Sarva Sewa Trust offers mentorship programs, fostering one-on-one connections between experienced individuals and students. Under the caring guidance of mentors, the students receive valuable insights, advice, and support to navigate the challenges of academic and personal growth.



Academic Lessons


The commitment to education at Sarva Sewa Trust extends to providing academic lessons and teaching sessions. Beyond traditional scholarship programs, the organization ensures that students receive a quality education through focused lessons, contributing to their overall academic excellence.



Sport Camps


Recognizing the importance of a holistic development approach, Sarva Sewa Trust encourages sports participation among the students. Sports activities not only promote physical fitness but also instill qualities of teamwork, discipline, and perseverance, contributing to the well-rounded growth of the beneficiaries.



Extra-Curricular Activities


Extracurricular activities are an integral part of Sarva Sewa Trust's approach to child development. From cultural events to skill-building workshops, students are provided with opportunities to explore their talents and interests beyond the academic realm, fostering a diverse range of skills and passions.



Counseling Sessions


Sarva Sewa Trust places a strong emphasis on mental health. Professional counseling services are readily available to address teenage issues, mental health concerns, and depression. This proactive approach ensures that the emotional well-being of the students is prioritized alongside their academic journey. They are also taught to be kind, empathetic, and encouraged to give back to society in whatever capacity they can.



>

Dental Camps


In an effort to enhance healthcare accessibility, Sarva Sewa Trust organizes dental camps. These camps not only cater to the dental health needs of the students but extend their services to their families as well. The trust ensures that dental care is provided free of charge, underscoring its commitment to the overall health of the community.



Eye Camps


The Sarva Sewa Trust extends its healthcare initiatives to eye camps, providing essential eye care services to students and their families. Prescription eyewear, including powered glasses, is offered free of charge, ensuring that visual impairments do not hinder the educational pursuits of the beneficiaries. The trust's focus on eye care contributes to the overall well-being of the community it serves. Further, cataract operations for older family members are provided at heavily subsidized rates.

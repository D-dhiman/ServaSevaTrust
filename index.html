Our Vision

What originated as a scholarship initiative has blossomed into a comprehensive approach to the development of children. Beyond financial support, the trust now offers mentorship, medical camps, extracurricular activities, and academic sessions. Acknowledging the multifaceted challenges faced by today's youth, the NGO extends its support to address teenage issues, mental health, and depression. Professional assistance from psychologists is readily available to ensure the holistic well-being of the students.


In addition to the focus on mental health, Sarva Sewa Trust provides access to essential healthcare through eye camps and dental camps. These initiatives not only cater to the students but also extend their reach to the families, offering medical facilities at no cost. In instances where prescription eyewear is required, the trust ensures that powered glasses are provided without imposing financial burdens. This comprehensive approach underscores the organization's commitment to the overall welfare of the communities it serves.

Importantly, Sarva Sewa Trust is officially registered with the government, solidifying its commitment to transparency and accountability in its operations.

Over the years, Sarva Sewa Trust has made a significant impact, aiding over 10,000 children in their educational pursuits. The tangible success stories of the alumni, who have ventured into diverse fields, reflect the trust's enduring commitment to empowering individuals. These alumni now serve as chartered accountants, police officers, Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officers, bank managers, and professionals across various domains, embodying the organization's dedication to shaping a brighter future for the next generation.
